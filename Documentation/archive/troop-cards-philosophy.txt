? Deck-Building Rules
    • Each player picks 4 cards, with these restrictions for balance:
        ? 1 Ground Tank (Knight, Hydra, Giant, Demon)
        ? 1 Air or Hybrid Unit (Dragon, Serpent, Valkyrie)
        ? 1 Ranged or Magic (Wizard, Archer)
        ? 1 Flex / Support / Assassin (Infernal Soul, Shadow Assassin, Cleric)
    • Mana total per deck should be ? 22 (average of ~5.5 each).

? Design Philosophy
    • Power normalized by cost: high-cost units are stronger but slower and rarer.
    • Biome effects give tactical edges — not total dominance.
    • Speed = tactical reach: slower units can still control space via defense or area attacks.
