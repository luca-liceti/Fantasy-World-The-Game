Menu Navigation
Button hover
Faint, dry paper-tap or soft wooden thud — like a finger brushing parchment
Button click / confirm
Decisive single knock, like a chess piece placed down — satisfying, not heavy
Back / cancel
Lighter, slightly reversed version of the confirm click — a gentle retraction
Menu open
Short low whoosh of air, like a heavy book being opened
Menu close
Inverse of menu open — slightly softer, air compressing shut
Tab switch
Quick dry click, like flipping a worn card on a table
Slider drag
Continuous soft scraping, like a wooden bead sliding along a track
Toggle on
Clean snapping click — mechanical, brief
Toggle off
Same snap but slightly lower pitch — feels like releasing tension
Dropdown open
Faint rustle, like leafing through pages
Dropdown select
Quiet definitive tap, as if placing a finger on a specific entry
Background crossfade
No sound — visuals only (silence is intentional for the background cycle)
Deck Selection
Card hover

Card select
Heavier, resonant chime with a quick magical tail — card locking into place
Card deselect
Reversed card select — slightly hollow, the card being pulled back
Mana limit reached
Low warning hum, like a vibrating tuning fork held too close — not harsh, just firm
Invalid selection
Brief descending two-tone buzz — error, but medieval-flavored, not digital
Timer tick (last 10s)
Quiet ticking clock, like an antique pocket watch — escalates in volume only
Selection complete
Rising three-note ascending chime — done, anticipatory, like a tournament horn prep
Dice Roll
Throw button pressed
Hand-release whoosh — the dice leaving the grip with a quick air puff
Dice tumbling
Rapid irregular clacking on a hard wood surface — like real dice rolling across a table
Dice settle / stop
Final sharp clack followed by 0.5s of wobble decay — comes to rest naturally
Tie — re-roll
Low, unresolved musical sting — then goes back to throw sound
Winner declared
Single triumphant low horn note — short, weighty, not celebratory yet

Combat Resolution
Hit (normal)
Solid weapon-on-armor impact — defined by troop type, but generically: meaty thud
Miss / dodge
Quick air-cut whoosh with no impact — the weapon passing through empty space
Critical hit (18-20)
Explosive crack — the kind of hit that shakes the table; brief screen-flash is paired
Critical miss (natural 1)
Stumble-clatter — weapon dropping or footing lost, almost comedic in weight
Counter attack triggered
Sharp reversal — a quick riposte swipe sound, faster and tighter than a normal hit
Endure triggered
Heavy impact that almost breaks through — a desperate crunch followed by a gasping hold
Status effect applied
Distinct per type: Burn = crackling hiss; Poison = wet gurgle; Stun = electric zap; Slow = deep thud; Curse = hollow dark whisper; Terrify = sharp shriek; Root = vines snapping; Stealth = shadow whoosh
Status effect tick (per turn)
Shorter version of the apply sound — quieter, a reminder not a re-trigger
Status effect expires
Light dissipating tone — a pop or fade, relief-coded
Damage number appears
Tied to hit sound — no separate UI sound needed
Heal number appears
Soft ascending chime with a warm resonance — clearly different from damage
Type super effective
Resonant swell under the hit sound — the impact rings longer, like a tuned bell struck right
Type not very effective
Duller, shorter hit — like striking stone, the energy absorbed rather than transmitted
Immune
Complete deflection — a hollow dome sound, the attack passing harmlessly
Economy

Gold received (bounty / reward)
Fuller coin cascade — more coins, a brief ringing jingle, more celebratory
Not enough gold

XP gain

Max level reached
Full ascending chord that resolves — final, definitive, no more upgrades available

Item picked up
Quick collect chime — satisfying, fast, pocketable feeling

Item used

Notifications & Toasts
Toast appear (neutral)
Short, clean pop — like a notification card being placed on the table
First Blood bounty
Sharp horn burst — quick, triumphant, almost war-horn in miniature
Kill Streak bounty
Escalating version — each streak fires the same sound pitched slightly higher
Revenge Kill bounty
Dramatic resolution chord — the "justified" feeling, emotionally satisfying
Mine Raider bounty
Coin burst + crash hybrid — stealing and destroying at once
Enemy troop eliminated

End of Match
Victory
Full triumphant fanfare — brass-led, resolving upward, 5–8 seconds, loopable tail
Defeat

Draw / tie
Unresolved chord that hangs — neither victory nor defeat, ambiguous, fades out
Stat card appear
Quiet paper-unfurl or scroll-unroll — each stat line appearing has a faint tick
Rematch confirm
Same as deck selection complete — rising three-note ascending chime, eager
